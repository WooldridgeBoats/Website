Canyon

INBOARD JET · 21 / 23 / 25 / 27 FT · CUSTOM DIMENSIONS ON REQUEST
Whitewater-bred inboard for big, technical rivers.

The Canyon's broad bow design is built for turbulent whitewater runs and brawling rapids — it provides vital lift and excellent control in fast-moving water, backed by an intensive support-structure system for durability in the harshest conditions. It's a favorite among those who venture well off the beaten track.

Standard configuration is a large walk-through windshield for easy access and maximum visibility from the helm; an open center console concept is also available. Options run deep: enclosed cabin, Idaho deck, even stick steer. On 23' boats and larger (without cabin), our popular "fishing machine" option moves the engine forward two feet with full walk-around access and adds two under-floor 50-gallon saddle tanks for extra-long excursions.

Full-length welded side trays with marine carpet, stand-up gunnels with toe kick, and a transom sink/fish box with cutting board round out the fishing package. The standard pump is the Hamilton 214 with turbo impeller, with the full range of gas and diesel engines available. Limited Lifetime Hull Warranty to the original buyer.
