River Rat — DIY Kit

KIT BOAT · BUILD IT YOURSELF
Build your own Wooldridge, cut and kitted by us.

The River Rat is a Wooldridge you weld yourself: a complete hull kit, precision-cut at our factory from the same alloy we build every boat with, shipped with the drawings and guidance to put it together right.

It's the most affordable way into a Wooldridge hull — and the most satisfying, if you're handy with a welder. Contact the factory for current kit pricing and lead times.
