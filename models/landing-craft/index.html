Landing Crafts

WORKBOAT · CUSTOM QUOTED
Bow-ramp workboats for cargo, crew, and remote access.

Wooldridge landing crafts put a full-width bow ramp on a welded-aluminum hull built to take a beach landing every day of its working life. They move equipment, materials, and crews to places without docks — remote cabins, island properties, job sites, and research stations.

Every landing craft is a custom build: deck layout, ramp dimensions, power (outboard, inboard jet, or offshore bracket), and cabin configuration are all specified to the job. Contact the factory to start a quote.
