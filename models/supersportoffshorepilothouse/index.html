Super Sport Offshore Pilothouse

OFFSHORE · SSO HULL
Fully enclosed helm for all-season running.

The SSO Pilothouse puts a fully enclosed helm on the Super Sport Offshore hull — heat, dry storage, and protected visibility for running big water in every season. It's the boat for owners whose calendar doesn't stop when the weather turns.

Pilothouse builds are configured and quoted with our team on the SSO platform — start from the Super Sport Offshore in Build & Price, then talk to us about the Pilothouse configuration.
