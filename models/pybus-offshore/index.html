Pybus Offshore

OFFSHORE · CUSTOM QUOTED
Expedition-grade offshore hull, Alaska-proven.

The Pybus Offshore is our expedition-grade offshore platform — named for Alaska's Pybus Bay and built for the kind of water that surrounds it. A deep, heavily structured welded-aluminum hull carries big fuel, big payload, and all-weather protection for long-range fishing and cruising in the North Pacific.

Pybus builds are quoted directly by the factory and configured per owner. Contact us to talk through your mission.
