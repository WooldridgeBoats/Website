Deepwater Series

OFFSHORE · CUSTOM QUOTED
Heavy offshore platforms for serious blue water.

The Deepwater Series is our heavy offshore line — larger welded-aluminum platforms engineered for open-ocean conditions, extended range, and serious payloads. Each Deepwater build is configured around its mission: sportfishing, expedition cruising, or working duty.

Deepwater boats are quoted directly by the factory. Tell us where you run and what you carry, and we'll spec the hull, power, and layout to match.
