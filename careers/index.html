Come build boats with us.

SEATTLE, WASHINGTON · FOUR-GENERATION FAMILY SHOP

Wooldridge Boats is a four-generation family shop in Seattle building 70 to 140 boats a year. We hire people who take pride in work that outlives them: welders, fabricators, riggers, finishers, and service techs.

If you want to build boats that run the hardest water in North America, we want to hear from you.
