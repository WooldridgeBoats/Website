WOOLDRIDGE  -  PHOTO PATHWAYS
Drag-and-drop staging for every model's website photos.
=======================================================================

WHAT THIS IS
The models are split into four groups at the top level:

   1 - OUTBOARDS
   2 - INBOARDS
   3 - OFFSHORE
   4 - OTHER BUILDS      (Landing Craft and Agency builds)

Open a group and you'll find its models, each in its own numbered
folder. Whatever photo you drop into a slot is the photo the website
will use for that job. You are in full control - nothing is picked for
you.


HOW EACH MODEL FOLDER IS LAID OUT
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Open any model, for example:

   1 - OUTBOARDS  ->  01 - ALASKAN LT - 16, 18, 20 FT

Inside you'll find:

   00 - MODEL CARD AND HERO
        CARD THUMBNAIL (CHOOSE 1)
             The single photo used on the "Our Boats" grid card for this
             model. Your cleanest, best side-on profile. ONE photo.
        MODEL HERO (CHOOSE 1)
             The big banner photo across the top of the model's page.
             Wide / landscape shots look best here. ONE photo.

   16 FT   /   18 FT   /   20 FT      (one folder per length offered)
        1 - HERO (CHOOSE 1)
             Lead photo for THIS length's section of the gallery. ONE.
        2 - FEATURED (CHOOSE 3-8)
             Your hand-picked highlights for this length. These are shown
             first and large. A handful (about 3 to 8) is perfect.
        3 - FLUFF - EVERYTHING ELSE
             Every other good photo of this length. Drop as many as you
             like - this is the deep gallery.


WHAT TO DROP IN
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
 -  JPGs straight off the shoot are ideal. Landscape orientation reads
    best on the site.
 -  Heroes: the wider the better (think banner). Cards: clean side
    profile on a simple background.
 -  Big files are fine. Everything is automatically resized for the web
    when it goes live - drop full-quality originals.
 -  Filenames do NOT matter. Name them anything; the website ignores the
    names and uses the FOLDER to decide each photo's job.
 -  You do not have to fill every slot. Empty folders are simply skipped.
 -  Put a length's best 1-2 shots in "FEATURED" and the rest in "FLUFF";
    don't overthink it - anything in "FLUFF" still shows in the gallery.


WHEN YOU'RE DONE  (or done with a model)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Just tell Stephen. Repointing the website at this folder is automatic on
our end - each photo lands in exactly the role and length you dropped it
into. You can hand it over model-by-model; it doesn't have to be all at
once.
