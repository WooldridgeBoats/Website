Go Farther. Go Skinny. Go Wooldridge.

For over a century, Wooldridge Boats has set the standard for innovation on the water. Founded in 1915 by Glen Wooldridge, the company was born from a relentless drive to explore rivers few had ever dared to run—and to build boats capable of getting there.

Over 110 Years of Innovation

From the Rogue River to Alaska’s Yukon, California’s Klamath, Idaho’s most formidable waterways, and the untouched rivers of British Columbia, Glen’s legacy is defined by “first-evers.” Today, based in Seattle, Washington, Wooldridge Boats continues that legacy—designing and building USA-made, high-performance aluminum and HDPE boats.
